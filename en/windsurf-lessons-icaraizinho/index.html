Why learn windsurf in Icaraizinho?

Icaraizinho de Amontada, in Ceará, is one of the best windsurf spots in the world. The northeast wind blows in the perfect direction — side-on shore — guaranteeing safe and ideal conditions for learning and progressing. The warm water (27°C), long beaches and shallow lagoon make learning faster and more enjoyable for all levels.

At Todos Al Agua we're the only school in Icaraizinho that includes transport: we pick you up at your accommodation with our 4×4 vehicles and bring you back after the lesson. You arrive empty-handed and leave riding.

Windsurf Lesson Spots in Icaraizinho

We select the best spot for each lesson based on tide, wind and the student's level. You don't have to worry — our team chooses and takes you. We use Icaraizinho Bay, Praia da Ponta in Moitas and Lagoa dos Patos to run lessons. Each location offers special conditions at different tide levels.
